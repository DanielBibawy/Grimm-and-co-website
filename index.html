WELCOME TO GRIMM & CO

Grimm & Co’s Apothecary to the Magical was established in 1148 by our celebrated founder, Master Graham Grimm.

Recently Graham made the groundbreaking decision to make our Rotherham apothecary visible to all beings (including mortals).
